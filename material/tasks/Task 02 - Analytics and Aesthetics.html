# Lab - Scalable Applications These tasks provide an opportunity for you to try out and experiment with the D3 features (e.g., custimizing and modifying basic examples to specific problems and data sets). # Chernoff Faces The Chernoff Faces method is a data visualization technique brought to us by the 70's. It was developed by Herman Chernoff to represent multivariate data, ostensibly effectively representing up to 18 variables. Facial features(eyes, nose, eyebrows) are mapped to multiple variables, with size, orientation, shape, color, and placement potentially representing different attributes of a single observation.
